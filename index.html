Advice AI

semper ubi sub ubi

How to use

  1. In the first text box describe your situation and ask a question. For example: "I am sad. What should I do?"
  2. Optionally enter your age in the second box
  3. Press Ask to get advice
  4. If you don't like the advice: Click Ask Again!

The background

This is the combined effort of myself, Zaki, and Brandon. You can find our code on Github.

I originally got the idea from an interaction I had with my high school Psychology teacher Mr. Copeland. He gave me red solo cups and told me to do one nice thing for someone. This is the thing!
